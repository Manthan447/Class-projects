  • our new president

    As you all know that this year entire world is facing a completely unprecedented crisis due to the spread of the Novel Corona Virus and at the same time our beloved President Shree Rajnikant Shah Sir has passed away. At this time of fear and sorrow, a light came up to protect us in form of our “New President Sir Mr. Vanil Shah”. Yes, Ladies and Gentlemen, we are very happy and proud to announce to you that Mr. Vanil Shah Sir is the new President of Royal International School and Dombivli Yuvak Education Trust who brought a lot of happiness, positivity, courage, motivation, growth, and development for Royal International School. A man from a new generation with lots of new and innovative ideas and with lots of positivity who inspires us to grow and to fly high. As a result under his guidance, supervision, and monitoring we have achieved CBSE Affiliation for Senior Secondary Level i.e. up to XII Std. Now Royal International School became the first CBSE affiliated school till Senior Secondary Level in the entire Dombivli region. We all are obliged to work under his guidance and supervision as the President of Royal International School
